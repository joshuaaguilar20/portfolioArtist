About Me

As a child, my brother would take art lessons and I would sit in the galleries of the Crocker Art Museum during his classes. It was an awe inspiring place for a five year old. I loved the color and majesty of the museum.

Always my favorite subject in school, I received a degree in fine art from the University of California, Davis. I also studied at Sacramento City College and UC San Diego.I have made art throughout the years and shown in various galleries in California and Nevada. I now have a renewed energy and focus and enjoy every hour spent painting.h.
